Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your personal information.

Last Updated: September 23, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Islamic Will Creator is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This tool operates entirely in your browser, ensuring maximum privacy and security for your sensitive inheritance and family information.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only: Our Islamic Will Creator operates entirely in your web browser using local storage technology. This means:

  • No Server Storage: Your will data, inheritance calculations, and personal information are never sent to our servers
  • Local Browser Storage: All information is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage and sessionStorage
  • Your Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time
  • No Registration Required: We don't collect email addresses, names, or any registration information

What Data is Stored Locally

The following information may be stored in your browser's local storage:

  • Will Information: Personal details, family structure, assets, and will preferences you enter
  • Calculation Data: Inheritance calculations and heir information
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (page views, feature usage) for improving the tool
  • Preferences: Your settings and preferences for using the tool
  • Session Data: Temporary data during your current session

What We Don't Collect

We are committed to not collecting the following:

  • Personal Identifiers: No names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
  • Financial Information: No bank details, account numbers, or financial institutions
  • Location Data: No GPS coordinates, IP addresses, or location tracking
  • Device Information: No unique device identifiers or detailed device information
  • Biometric Data: No fingerprints, facial recognition, or biometric identifiers
  • Social Media Data: No social media profiles or connections

Analytics and Usage Tracking

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service:

  • Anonymous Metrics: Page views, feature usage, and general usage patterns
  • No Personal Data: Analytics data cannot be linked to individual users
  • Browser-Based: All analytics are processed locally in your browser
  • Optional Third-Party: You may choose to enable Google Analytics or other services
  • Opt-Out Available: You can disable analytics tracking in your browser settings

Information Sharing

We do not share your information with anyone because:

  • No Server Access: We don't have access to your data since it's stored locally
  • No Third Parties: We don't sell, rent, or share user data with third parties
  • No Government Requests: We cannot provide data to governments because we don't have it
  • No Marketing: We don't use your data for marketing or advertising purposes
  • No Profiling: We don't create user profiles or behavioral tracking

Cookies and Tracking

Our approach to cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies Only: We only use cookies necessary for the tool to function
  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use tracking or advertising cookies
  • Local Storage: We primarily use HTML5 localStorage instead of cookies
  • Session Management: Temporary session cookies may be used for functionality
  • Third-Party Cookies: We don't embed third-party tracking cookies

Data Security

How we protect your information:

  • Client-Side Processing: All data processing happens in your browser
  • HTTPS Encryption: All communications use secure HTTPS encryption
  • No Data Transmission: Sensitive data never leaves your device
  • Browser Security: We rely on your browser's built-in security features
  • Regular Updates: We keep our code updated with latest security practices

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in your browser's local storage
  • Modification: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Clear all data using browser settings or our clear data function
  • Export: Download your data in JSON or PDF format
  • Portability: Move your data to another device by exporting and importing

Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy:

  • Age Restriction: This tool is intended for adults 18+ years old
  • No Children's Data: We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • Parental Guidance: Children should use this tool only with parental supervision
  • Educational Purpose: Any use by minors should be for educational purposes only

International Users

Global privacy protection:

  • GDPR Compliant: Meets European Union privacy requirements
  • CCPA Compliant: Meets California privacy law requirements
  • Global Standards: Follows international privacy best practices
  • Local Laws: Users are responsible for compliance with local privacy laws

Changes to Privacy Policy

How we handle policy updates:

  • Notification: Changes will be posted on this page with updated date
  • Material Changes: Significant changes will be prominently displayed
  • Continued Use: Using the tool after changes constitutes acceptance
  • Version History: Previous versions available upon request

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Technical Questions: Use the feedback form in the main application
  • Privacy Concerns: Contact us through the feedback system
  • Legal Inquiries: Consult with legal professionals in your jurisdiction

Remember: Since we don't collect personal data, we may not be able to respond to individual privacy requests, but we're committed to maintaining the highest privacy standards.
